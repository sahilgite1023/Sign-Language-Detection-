Sign Language Recognition

Real-time American Sign Language recognition using advanced AI technology.

Our Mission

SignSense is dedicated to making sign language recognition accessible to everyone. We believe that communication should be barrier-free, and our technology helps bridge the gap between the hearing and deaf communities.

Our advanced AI-powered system can recognize American Sign Language (ASL) gestures in real-time, providing instant feedback and improving communication for users of all abilities.

How It Works

The technology behind our sign language recognition

1

Capture

Our system captures your hand gestures through your device's camera in real-time.

2

Process

Advanced computer vision algorithms detect and track hand landmarks and movements.

3

Recognize

Our deep learning model analyzes the hand positions and identifies the corresponding sign language letter.
